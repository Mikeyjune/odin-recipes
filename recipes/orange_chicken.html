Orange Chicken

Home

Description

One of my personal favorite dishes of all time! Consisting of small chunks of chicken that are breaded, pan fried, and coated in a savory orange sauce, it's a quick and easy meal that has yet to let me down. A fun fact about this dish: while it is widely popular in Chinese restaurants across the U.S., it's actually not traditional Chinese cuisine.

Ingredients


Orange Sauce

  • 1 Orange for zest
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Chicken

  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups vegetable oil (frying)
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 large shallot (chopped)

Rice

  • white rice OR fried rice

Garnish (Optional)

  • Sliced scallions
  • Sesame seeds
